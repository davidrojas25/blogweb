Coaching Methodology: Age appropriate mileage volume with gradual increases as runners mature in age and experience Periodic training cycles; both micro and macro cycles to fully develop young runners to their potential Strong Base building without neglecting all 6 (yes 6) running systems Hills Hills Hills Aerobic Threshold is key to endurance runners Proper running form needs to be developed at an early age Nutrition is a hidden gem that can help runners go to another level ​Sports Psychology must be part of any training program .
