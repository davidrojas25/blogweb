General Information about Whittier Warriors Youth Running

Who are we:

We are a youth running team dedicated to promoting the sport of running to kids ages 6-14. We off various levels and focus so that any kids regardless of ability or age can build the love for our sport! We run year round with 2 season which include Cross Country in the fall and Distance Track in the spring. We hope you can join us and become part of our Warrior family
